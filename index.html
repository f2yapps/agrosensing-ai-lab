About

I am a Post-Doctoral Research Associate at the University of Maryland, working in precision and digital agriculture. My research focuses on UAV-based imaging, machine learning and deep learning for plant phenotyping, soil moisture prediction, evapotranspiration estimation, and sustainable agricultural water management.
