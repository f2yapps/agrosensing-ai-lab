Research Projects

Postdoctoral Research (University of Maryland & Purdue)

  • Collected multi-depth soil samples and multispectral drone imagery from organic farms across Indiana to support state-level soil health assessments.
  • Active member of the Indiana Organic Network (ION), a USDA NIFA-funded initiative to establish a statewide network of organic farmers for knowledge sharing, community building, and on-farm research.
  • Developed a project proposal on “Leveraging A Physics-Informed AI Model and UAV Imaging for Monitoring Soil Water Dynamics to Support Precision Farm Management” for a Purdue University internal grant.
  • Attended a week-long UAV-Based Phenotyping Data Analysis Workshop (GRYFN system: RGB, LiDAR, hyperspectral sensors and software) at Purdue University.
  • Employed soil proximal and UAV sensors (Alpha II FT-IR Spectrometer, Veris U-series Organic Matter sensors) and collected soil samples for soil organic carbon spatiotemporal variability mapping in Indiana.
  • Developed lab protocols and gave training on operating U3000 Veris OM sensor, FAA Part 107 drone license preparation, Negishi HPC, and ethical use of LLMs (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, DeepSeek, Meta LlaMA) for research.
  • Comprehensive review on progress, challenges, and future directions in soil organic carbon monitoring using AI, UAVs, and satellite remote sensing.
  • Two-day workshop on operating the Negishi HPC cluster (Rosen Center for Advanced Computing, Purdue University).
  • Advised graduate students’ theses on field-level soil organic carbon modeling using geospatial data and machine learning.
  • Guest lectures on UAV-based imaging for plant phenotyping and precision water management, and on leveraging advanced technologies and AI for sustainable agroecosystems.

Ph.D. Research (University of Florida)

  • Evaluated UAV-based multispectral imaging and machine learning for sweet corn phenotyping.
  • Investigated green bean and sweet corn responses to full and deficit irrigation; evaluated DSSAT performance in simulating crop growth and yield under different irrigation levels.
  • Simulated crop evapotranspiration and soil moisture for green bean and sweet corn under full and deficit irrigation using DSSAT.
  • Evaluated machine learning and deep learning algorithms for soil moisture prediction under different irrigation levels.
  • Investigated machine learning algorithms and calibrated empirical models for reference evapotranspiration estimation in DSSAT.
  • Evaluated hydrological and machine learning models for field-scale soil hydrology in calcareous soils (South Florida) for VRI-irrigated sweet corn and green bean.
  • Evaluating ground and UAV-based thermal sensors for canopy temperature–based crop evapotranspiration estimation.
  • Collected thermal and multispectral images over four years using DJI Matrices 210 V2, IF1200A, Mavic 3 Multispectral drones, Zenmuse XT2 Thermal, and MicaSense RedEdge-MX Multispectral sensor.
  • Operated precision agriculture technologies: sprinkler/VRI irrigation, TEROS 12/21, HYPROP WP4C, pressure bomb, Richard’s Pressure Plate, Tempe cell, weather and IRT sensors, FLIR camera, SPAD, CIRAS 2 & 3, Porometer.
  • Analyzed UAV and satellite imagery using Pix4D, ArcGIS, ERDAS, ENVI, QGIS, Python, and RStudio.
  • Skilled in big data analytics, HiPerGator, machine learning, and deep learning (Python, RStudio).
  • Developed an automated UAV thermal and multispectral image processing pipeline for precision agriculture.

M.Th. Research (Banaras Hindu University)

  • GIS processing, digital image processing, optical/microwave/radar interpretation, photogrammetry, cartography, remote sensing, surface water modeling (MIKE 11 NAM, SWAT), climate change analysis, land use/land cover change, irrigation scheduling, drought characterization, land surveying (GPS).
  • Worked with Landsat 4–5/7/8, MODIS, Sentinel 2, LISS-3, soil top sheets, and ASTER GDEM.
  • Thematic extraction, geometric/radiometric correction, mosaicking, scan line error correction, subsetting, supervised and unsupervised classification.
  • Geo-referencing in ERDAS, QGIS, ArcGIS, ENVI, Python; vegetation indices, accuracy assessment, DEM and 3D creation.
  • PGDP and shapefile creation, on-screen digitization, topology, spatial modeling, network analysis, TIN/DEM, DGPS and total station survey.
